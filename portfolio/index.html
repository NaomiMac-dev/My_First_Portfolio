Projects

HERSAFE HAVEN - Empowering Girls With Safe Digital Spaces.

HerSafe Haven is a digital-first mentorship and emergency response platform designed to protect and empower girls across Kenya. The project combines AI technology, human mentorship, and a secure interface to offer emotional support, guidance and immediate help in crisis situations. Every girl deserves to fell safe, heard and guided no matter where she is.
